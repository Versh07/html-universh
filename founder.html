About the Founder

At UNIVERSH LIMITED, we recognize Jesus Christ Himself as our true Founder. Guided by His principles of love, compassion, and service, our mission is deeply rooted in faith and dedicated to uplifting those in need. Mr. Orjime Benjamin humbly serves as an overseer, stewarding the organization’s vision to bring hope and humanitarian aid to vulnerable communities across the globe. With Christ as our foundation, we are committed to making a lasting impact and spreading His love through our actions.

Orjime Benjamin is a driven and passionate individual from Nigeria, specifically from Benue State, Makurdi Local Government Area. Born in July 1987, he is currently based in the Federal Capital Territory (FCT) Abuja, Nigeria.

As a graduate with a Bachelor of Science degree in Chemistry, Benjamin has built a diverse and robust professional profile over the years. His experience spans multiple industries, including manufacturing, education, technical, and analytical fields. His technological know-how, coupled with a keen interest in handwork, solidifies his versatile skill set.

A man of faith, Benjamin is deeply rooted in his Christian beliefs and loves serving in God's vineyard. His faith plays a significant role in guiding both his personal and professional pursuits.

With a deep passion for serving humanity and restoring hope to the hopeless, Benjamin is building the foundation of a Social Impact Organization (Non-Public Sector), which is still in its early stages of establishment. This venture reflects his commitment to empowering others and creating a meaningful impact on society. Above all, he trusts in God as the true CEO of the organization, Universh Limited.

Beyond his work, Benjamin enjoys spending his leisure time on personal hobbies such as coding, watching movies, traveling, and listening to music. His diverse interests reflect his curiosity and enthusiasm for learning and experiencing the world in different ways.

Driven by a mission to simplify life, empower others, and inspire positive change, Benjamin sees his life’s purpose as extending beyond personal achievements. He is committed to contributing to the betterment of society, aspiring to be not just a professional but a leader who values community development and holistic growth.

In pursuit of societal improvement, Benjamin recently added web development to his skill set, enabling him to empower youth with technological expertise. This initiative equips young people to become experts in their fields, be it through self-employment, organizational roles, or mentoring others for a brighter future.
