About Us

UNIVERSH LIMITED, established in 2024, is a globally oriented, non-governmental organization committed to transforming lives through targeted humanitarian assistance. Our organization operates on the core belief that everyone deserves a life of dignity, health, and hope, regardless of their background, circumstances, or location. We have dedicated our resources and expertise to providing life-saving support to poor and vulnerable populations worldwide, with an unwavering commitment to serving humanity through compassion and integrity.

Our mission is to bridge gaps in healthcare, essential resources, and social support systems, ensuring that no one is left behind in times of need. From addressing urgent medical needs to supporting long-term community development, our actions are focused on creating sustainable solutions that empower individuals and uplift entire communities. We believe that by tackling both immediate challenges and their root causes, we can help build a future where everyone has equal access to the essentials of life.

Our Objectives

Our work is driven by five core objectives that guide our efforts and help us focus on meaningful, long-term change:

  1. Deliver Humanitarian Assistance and Relief

    2. To respond swiftly and effectively to crises, disasters, and urgent needs by providing food, shelter, medical aid, and other essential resources. Our aim is to alleviate suffering and restore hope to those affected by both natural and human-made challenges.

  2. Promote Accessible Healthcare Services

    3. We strive to improve healthcare access for marginalized communities by supporting medical facilities, providing essential medications, and offering health education. By focusing on preventive and primary care, we aim to reduce health disparities and improve quality of life.

  3. Empower Vulnerable Communities through Education and Skills Training

    4. We believe education and skills development are powerful tools for breaking the cycle of poverty. Through training programs, scholarships, and resources, we empower individuals to become self-sufficient and contribute positively to their communities.

  4. Support Sustainable Development Initiatives

    5. Our organization is committed to sustainable practices that address the root causes of poverty and vulnerability. From promoting clean water projects to supporting sustainable agriculture, we work with local communities to develop lasting solutions for a healthier planet.

  5. Encourage Partnerships for Greater Impact

    6. We recognize that collaboration is key to achieving our mission. By forming strategic partnerships with local governments, international organizations, and community leaders, we expand our reach and amplify our impact, making a difference in more lives than we could alone.

What We Do

At UNIVERSH LIMITED, we take a holistic approach to humanitarian aid, recognizing the interconnectedness of health, education, and socioeconomic stability. Our initiatives are designed to meet immediate needs while building resilient communities for the future. We operate across various sectors, including:

  • Healthcare Services:

    • From supporting hospitals in underserved areas to funding medical missions, we work to ensure people have access to the health services they need.

  • Educational Support:

    • We fund educational programs, provide scholarships, and facilitate skill-building workshops to promote self-reliance and economic empowerment.

  • Assist Widows and Orphanage Homes:

    • Provide financial and material support to widows and orphanages, helping them meet their daily needs and empowering them with resources to thrive.
